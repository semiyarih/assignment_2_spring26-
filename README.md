## Working Through the Assignment

- Complete each question in order.  
- If a question is divided into multiple parts, complete **each part separately**.  
- After finishing a question (or each part of a question), you must:
  - **commit your work** with a clear, descriptive commit message  
  - **push your changes** to GitHub  

Frequent, incremental commits are expected and will be part of the grading criteria.

## Collaboration and Academic Integrity

You may **discuss ideas** with your classmates in this course. However:

- You **may not copy** code or written answers from another student.  
- You **may not share your code** with classmates.  
- You **may not copy** solutions generated by AI tools.

If two or more submissions contain identical or nearly identical work, **all involved students will receive a score of 0** for the assignment.
